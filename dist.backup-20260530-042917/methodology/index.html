How CampDeck works

This page outlines how we test products, cite sources, and keep shopping fields current. When we use affiliate links, we may earn a commission; that does not change the price you pay. See the affiliate disclosure and contact us if you spot something that looks out of date.

Our review process

  • Step 1

    Every item is taken on a minimum of 2 real camping trips before review — no backyard or hotel-room tests.

  • Step 2

    Tents are set up in wind and rain; sleeping bags are rated against actual temperature performance, not manufacturer claims.

  • Step 3

    Pack weight is measured on our own scale; volume claims are verified by packing the listed capacity in real gear.

  • Step 4

    We note pack-out durability: gear that fails after one season is marked accordingly and removed from recommendations.

  • Step 5

    Budget picks are taken as seriously as premium picks — we name the cheapest option that doesn't compromise safety or comfort.

  • Step 6

    Affiliate links go to lowest price across REI, Amazon, and manufacturer direct; we note price-match policies.

# campdeck. Style Kit

## Brand Summary
campdeck is an app and affiliate brand for car-campers, weekend backpackers, and overlanders. The identity is practical, outdoorsy, and quietly reverent without being precious.

**Tagline:** Gear-tested weekends, packed smarter.

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| primary | #1F4E3D |
| secondary | #3D434A |
| accent | #E89B4A |
| background | #F4F0E8 |
| text | #222629 |
| muted | #6F756E |

Body-text contrast: `#222629` on `#F4F0E8` = **13.41:1**.

## Type Pairing Recommendation
Wordmark/display: Manrope, Space Grotesk, Comfortaa, or rounded utilitarian sans. UI/body: Inter or Roboto for checklists and gear tables.

## Voice & Tone Notes
Matter-of-fact, gear-tested, and calm. Respect the outdoors, but keep the advice direct and useful.

## Do
- Use pine, basalt, sunrise amber, and birch white.
- Lead with packing clarity and tested field usefulness.
- Keep imagery clean, foggy, and trailhead-ready.

## Don't
- Do not use extreme-sports energy.
- Do not overromanticize gear into lifestyle fluff.
- Do not make the system look like a hunting or tactical brand.
